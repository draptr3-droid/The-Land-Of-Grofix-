The World of Aethermoor

In an age before memory, the god-smith Vaelthar shattered the Primordial Crystal, scattering divine essence across the mortal realm. These shards — called Aetherstones — became the foundation of all magic, all conflict, and all destiny.

Five great civilizations rose to prominence: the iron-willed Drakthari Empire, the mystical Sylvani Accord, the subterranean Stoneheart Compact, the seafaring Tidecrown Kingdoms, and the wandering Ashborn Confederacy.

Now, a shadow stirs in the east. The Veilbreaker Cult seeks to reunite the shards and unmake creation itself. Alliances fracture. Heroes rise — and fall. The fate of Aethermoor rests not on prophecy, but on choice.
